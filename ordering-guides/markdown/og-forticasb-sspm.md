FortiCASB-SSPM

Unified Cybersecurity Across SaaS and

Third-Parties

In today’s modern SaaS-driven enterprise, organizations depend on dozens - often hundreds
or thousands - of SaaS applications and their interconnected third parties to power business
operations. But this growing SaaS stack introduces major blind spots. Third-party integrations,
unmanaged identities, and misconfigured apps have become prime entry points for attackers,
exposing sensitive data and disrupting operations. Traditional security tools lack the depth and
agility to manage this new threat landscape.

FortiCASB-SSPM is purpose-built to secure the entire SaaS stack - applications, users, and third-
party connections - through an automated, risk-based platform that integrates seamlessly into
enterprise environments. The product enables organizations to identify risks earlier, respond faster,
and enforce consistent policy without slowing down the business.

Shadow and Third-Party Discovery

FortiCASB-SSPM discovers all SaaS assets in the organization, including managed and unmanaged
(Shadow SaaS) applications, and maps third-party integrations. This unified discovery provides the
foundation for risk assessment, compliance, and attack surface reduction.

SaaS Misconfiguration Management (SSPM)

Security misconfigurations are a leading cause of SaaS breaches. FortiCASB-SSPM continuously
monitors configuration drift across enterprise SaaS apps, detects deviations from baselines, and
enforces best practices. It ensures SaaS platforms remain hardened and compliant with security
frameworks.

Data Security Posture Management (DSPM)

FortiCASB-SSPM provides full visibility and control over sensitive data across SaaS applications. It
automatically classifies and identifies exposure risks such as public file shares, over-permissioned
users, and unencrypted data. By correlating data context with configuration and identity posture, it
prioritizes remediation actions based on business impact and compliance requirements.

Identity Posture Management

FortiCASB-SSPM enhances visibility into identity posture across SaaS environments. It detects
unused, risky, or misconfigured accounts, enforces SSO and MFA, integrates local users into the
enterprise IdP, and removes dormant accounts to reduce identity-based risk.

GenAI Application Management

With the rise of generative AI tools, organizations must establish usage controls. FortiCASB-SSPM identifies and governs GenAI
applications, monitors token-based access, and enforces policies to mitigate data leakage and compliance risks associated with
AI usage.

Compliance Alignment

Built-in mappings to leading security frameworks — including SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST 800-53, MITRE ATT&CK for SaaS, GDPR,
and HIPAA — allow continuous compliance monitoring and posture scoring across SaaS environments.

Remediation and Automation Workflows

Automated, no-code workflows empower teams to address misconfigurations and identity risks efficiently. Integrated ticketing
systems and approval workflows engage business owners directly, enabling fast, auditable remediation while minimizing
operational friction.

Deployment and Integration

FortiCASB-SSPM integrates through secure, API-based connections using OAuth 2.0 and SSO authentication. It requires no
agents or traffic rerouting, ensuring rapid onboarding and minimal disruption. Integration with FortiAnalyzer, FortiGate, and the
Fortinet Security Fabric enables unified analytics, reporting, and policy enforcement.

Support and Service Levels

All subscriptions include FortiCare Premium Support and continuous posture monitoring.

In Summary

FortiCASB-SSPM delivers a unified approach to SaaS security, providing end-to-end protection for applications, identities, and
third-party connections. Through automation, risk prioritization, and complete visibility, FortiCASB-SSPM empowers security
teams to manage SaaS risk efficiently, enabling secure growth in an increasingly interconnected digital environment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS – FortiCASB-SSPM

Q: How is FortiCASB-SSPM licensed?

A: FortiCASB-SSPM is licensed based on the number of SaaS users in your environment. Tiers typically include 50-100,
101-1000, 1001-5000, 5001-10000, and 10,000+ users.

Q: Is FortiCASB-SSPM compliant with industry standards?

A: Yes, FortiCASB-SSPM has achieved SOC 2 Type 2 attestation and ISO 27001 certification.

Q: Do I need admin-level API access to integrate with SaaS applications?

A: In most cases, FortiCASB-SSPM integrates with read-only permissions. Less than 10% of connectors require admin-level
access for full functionality. For those, a reduced-functionality mode is also available.

Q: Is FortiCASB-SSPM a cloud-only solution?

A: Yes. FortiCASB-SSPM is a SaaS-native, agentless platform delivered as a cloud service. No on-prem or VM deployment is
required.

Q: Does FortiCASB-SSPM include remediation workflows?

A: Yes. The platform includes no-code remediation workflows that automate ticketing, access revocation, misconfiguration fixes,
and business justification steps.

Q: How is Generative AI usage monitored and controlled?

A: FortiCASB-SSPM detects GenAI apps through OAuth tokens, service accounts, and endpoint-level traffic. It allows security
teams to govern usage, revoke unauthorized tools, and enforce policy.

Q: What compliance frameworks does FortiCASB-SSPM support?

A: The platform maps risks and controls to leading frameworks including NIST CSF, SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and MITRE
ATT&CK, helping organizations maintain and demonstrate compliance.

Q: Can FortiCASB-SSPM integrate with SIEM, SOAR, and ITSM tools?

A: Yes. FortiCASB-SSPM supports integration with leading SIEM, SOAR, and ITSM platforms via REST APIs and webhooks. This
includes tools such as Splunk, QRadar, ServiceNow, and others. Fortinet-native integrations are also available for FortiSIEM and
FortiSOAR.

Q: Is there a minimum contract size or duration?

A: The minimum order tier starts at 50 users. Standard contract terms are 12, 24, or 36 months.
